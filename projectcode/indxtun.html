INDRAVARMA X TUNTUN

This is my first attempt at creating artwork. King Indravama is a character from the popular Indian cartoon Chhota Bheem, and Tuntun is another significant character from the same series. I wanted to explore a new perspective by reimagining them as a romantic couple.

The concept and design of Indravama are entirely my own, including the facial features and styling. Tuntun's concept, however, was inspired by an existing version I came across.

Before moving ahead, I request everyone to watch this video. You wil love it haha.

I don't have the outline for Indravama's art, but I first created the base and then worked on adding stuff, shading and all that.
